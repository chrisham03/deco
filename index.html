Experience Art Deco

Deco is the premier hotel for guests seeking to experience the opulence of the Art Deco era. Deco was originally opened in 1925, known then as The Eastern Hotel, and quickly became one of the most premier hotels in all of New York City.

Explore what Deco has to offer.

Eastern Theater

The historic Eastern Theater, opened with the hotel in 1925, is one of the most prestigious theaters in all of New York City. Deco guests are treated to complementary admission to the many amazing plays, musicals, and performances that take place at the Eastern Theater.

The Deco Eastern

The Deco Eastern is a 5 star restaurant. Our world-class chefs prepare a variety of amazing dishes of all kinds from around the world. The Deco Eastern is a one of a kind restaurant experience. Open daily from 10am-12am.
